Artist: Ziggy Marley

Song: Beach in Hawaii

This track by Ziggy, Bob Marley’s oldest son, is a hit off of his 2006 album “Love is my Religion” that won Reggae Album of the Year at the 2007 Grammys. Absolutely love the theme here; kicking back in the white sands of Maui with a babe and a Pina Colada, what could be better.

9/27/14

Artist: Damian Marley

Song: Welcome to Jamrock

“Out in the streets they call it murddaaaa!!!” This song was Damian’s hit on the simarily titled album “Welcome to Jamrock” in 2005. The youngest but most outspoken of Bob Marley’s sons, Damian details on the tough upbringing the average youth face in Jamaica. This track shows Damian’s ability of bringing unrivaled edge to the genre.

9/28/14

Artist: The Expendables

Song: Down Down Down

Another great example of why the Expendables are a dope Punk Reggae, Surf Reggae, Cali Reggae, whatever you call it, band. “Down Down Down” is a hit off of their self titled “The Expendables” album in 2007.

8/31/14

Artist: Tribal Seeds

Song: In Your Eyes

Let’s continue the Cali theme by introducing Tribal Seeds, a reggae band hailing from San Diego. Take a peek, Steven Jacobo boasts a unique (and jahhh) vibe on lead vocals.

8/30/14

Artist: Collie Buddz

Song: Tomorrow's Another Day

At All Day Reggae, we can promise you that the Collie Buddz picks will never cease. Another hit from the self titled album “Collie Buddz” from 2007. Love it more every time.

Artist: J Boog

Song: Coldest Zone

Finally our song of the week! J Boog absolutely killing it on this track. Of polynesian heritage, he is one of our favorite Island-style Reggae artists. “Coldest Zone” is the hit off of his self titled EP “J Boog” from 2011; we can promise you will be shouting “OOO Lord” in no time.
